ABOUT US

OUR VISION!

Our purpose is to develop a platform, a one stop solution for every student pursuing his/her studies in Commerce stream. In the modern world, where anything and everything seems to be a click away, we are about to bring all the relevant study material for UG courses in Commerce stream on one single platform.

OUR MISSION!

Since plenty of study material is available online for science students, it would be a helping hand for the commerce students to prepare well for their examinations with the help of text and video content for their respective courses. Keeping this in mind, we are looking forward to develop a web and mobile application that would help these students to have easy access to study material and resources prepared and verified by experienced personnel of the respective curriculum. It would definitely prove to be a step forward in direction of making our Digital India truly digital for all sections of educational department. Prepared notes and video lectures will definitely help the students to utilise their time in other productive things. We have a vision of making our education system more efficient and digitize the way of learning.
